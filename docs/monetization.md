# Sigma Core OS — Monetization Strategy

> This document outlines potential revenue models for Sigma Core OS and Sigma Futures.
> All trading-related features require compliance review before launch.

---

## Core Value Proposition

Sigma Core OS is a private agentic infrastructure for Sigma Futures that:
- Automates research and analysis workflows
- Assists with futures trading (with human approval)
- Accelerates software development via Sigma Dev
- Provides a foundation for productized AI agent services

---

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Internal Productivity (Immediate)
Use Sigma Core OS internally to reduce time spent on research, coding, and trading workflows.
This is direct cost savings, not revenue — but provides immediate ROI.

### 2. Agent-as-a-Service (Phase 3+)
Package individual agents as SaaS tools for other traders, developers, or firms:
- **Sigma Bot** — Subscription tier for futures research and alerts ($X/month)
- **Sigma Dev** — Coding assistant for quantitative developers
- **Sigma Research** — Market research subscriptions

### 3. Managed Agentic Infrastructure (Phase 4+)
Offer Sigma Core OS as a white-label infrastructure product:
- Other trading firms or fintech startups license the OS
- Custom agent deployments on their infrastructure
- Revenue model: licensing fee + usage-based pricing

### 4. Futures Trading Alpha (Internal, Long-term)
Sigma Bot assists in generating trading signals and executing strategies (with human oversight).
Revenue comes from trading P&L, not product sales.
- Requires full compliance review
- Requires paper trading phase validation
- Requires risk management policies to be implemented in `core/policies`

### 5. Data & Insights Products (Future)
Aggregate anonymized signals and research outputs into market intelligence products:
- Research reports
- Signal subscriptions
- Model performance dashboards

---

## Pricing Philosophy

- Start with internal use — no external sales until Phase 3
- Keep Sigma Bot free for internal use
- External tiers should be usage-based to align incentives
- Never automate money movement or trading without human approval gates

---

## Compliance Notes

- Trading-related features must be reviewed by a licensed compliance officer before public launch
- Sigma Bot must not autonomously execute trades in any paid tier
- All financial data handling must comply with applicable regulations (CFTC, SEC, etc.)
